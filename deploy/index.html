About the Oracle

Arizona Ball.
Nothing Else.

The Oracle is Arizona's most dedicated hub for AIA high school basketball. Built for the fans and obsessives who believe the state's talent deserves real coverage.

We dig deeper than the box score. AZ high school basketball coverage has barely scratched the surface — there's so much more data and insight to bring to the game, and that's what we're here to deliver. From player analysis to team breakdowns and rankings to transfer reactions and original podcasts and articles, we've got you covered.

Whether you're a player looking to get noticed, a parent tracking the scene, a coach doing your homework, or just a die-hard fan — The Oracle is your source.

Our Mission

Why the Oracle Exists

Arizona high school basketball deserves mainstream attention — and it doesn't get nearly enough. The Oracle exists to change that: to put the state's game in front of more people, pull more fans into the conversation, and shine a light on Arizona's next generation of hoopers.

There also just isn't enough data out there for the sport. That's the gap we're here to fill — with deeper analysis and extensive rating systems for every team in the state.

The name says it all: an oracle sees what others miss. We're here to surface it.
